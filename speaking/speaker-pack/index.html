Information for organisers

Some information about me that you might need to help plan your conference

My expectations of you

  • You will pay for any flights and hotel fees in advance of the event.
  • If your event is based outside of the EU, you will arrange for a visa for me to speak.
  • For my talk, I will use my own computer to control the slides. I will require my speaker notes to be visible to me during the talk.
  • I will not provide my slide deck in advance of the event because I am likely to make changes to it right before I speak, but I am happy to provide a link to my slides to attendees after the event.
  • Any footage recorded must not be sold without my permission after the event, this will require a separate agreement.
  • I will invoice you my fee after the event. The invoice must be paid in full in GBP within 14 days of receipt.
