Mozilla approached Clearleft to redesign the Firefox Addons site, which serves 2 million people per day. The site was built in a modular and well-documented way so that Mozilla's team of voluteers could implement it.

Paul Lloyd and Richard Rutter worked on the design, and I pair-programmed with Natalie Downe on the HTML and CSS.

Mozilla reported a 20% increase in traffic to the add-ons site after the new version went live.
