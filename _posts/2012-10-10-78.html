I've got an Evernote notebook full of the results of various tests I ran on game console browsers in preparation for my A List Apart article, and I'm currently working on a little site that makes these notes public.

There's not a huge amount of stuff in there yet and it's very rough, but I'll be adding more as I go. I'm recording things like the functions different buttons have in the browser, and what features are supported.

I'm looking forward to when the Wii U comes out, and IE9 for the Xbox. There should be a lot more interesting stuff to add then.
